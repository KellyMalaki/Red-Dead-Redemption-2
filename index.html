Welcome To The Wild West

Outlaws for life

America, 1899. The end of the Wild West era has begun. After a robbery goes badly wrong in the western town of Blackwater, Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang are forced to flee. With federal agents and the best bounty hunters in the nation massing on their heels, the gang must rob, steal and fight their way across the rugged heartland of America in order to survive. As deepening internal divisions threaten to tear the gang apart, Arthur must make a choice between his own ideals and loyalty to the gang who raised him.

You play as Arthur Morgan as him and his gang go through this difficult time of upcoming of civilization. You are provided with several choices through out the game which each choice that you make has it's own consiquence on the future. You are provided with responsibilities such as taking care of your horse which helps you move around the map, taking care of your weapons and providing for your gang by either hunting or robbing, whatever works for you. Red dead Redemption 2 has so much to offer. Activities that a player can do are almost endless. Are you ready for the challenge?
